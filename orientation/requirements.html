Requirements

  • Orientation is all online, via Discord! Discord is a free service, but requires an account to login. Students should have an account made prior to the start of their orientation week.
  • Reliable internet connection
  • Quiet learning environment with no distractions
  • Consistently reliable internet connection
  • Computer with a webcam and microphone
  • IOS recommendations: There is always some wiggle room here, but as a general rule, gaming is better on Windows computers. Although orientation is possible on Apple computers, we recommend Windows 7, 8, or 10 for the best experience across multiple titles.
  • PC specification recommendations:We use the Minecraft recommendations as a baseline. [click here]

Pricing

Orientation is a one-time, flat rate fee of $75 per student. After orientation is over, there will be no additional charges. Although we offer some incredible paid programs, our mission is to help create a better internet. We believe that starts with our kids. So to reduce as many barriers as possible, Hybrid membership is completely free after orientation! It should be easy to find a safe, friendly online space, and we hope that those values spread to the rest of the internet too.

Sample Day

  • 5:30 - 5:45pm: Stand Up

    Students check-in and build connections between other new students and their mentor.

  • 5:45-6:00pm: Gaming Education

    With so many games across so many genres, we believe there's a lot more to gaming than just the latest trend. So every day, students are introduced to a different game, learning a new set of skills and experiencing a new part of their favorite hobby.

  • 6:00-6:45pm: Gameplay

    With your class and mentor, learn new games first hand, or switch over to your favorite title!

  • 6:45-7:00pm Health Education

    The days of the "stereotypical" gamer are over. As a Hybrid, we learn to balance our online and offline life, and we learn how to take care of our bodies, minds, and relationships.
