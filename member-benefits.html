Redefining Gaming

Hybrid is a community of gamers who care. About themselves, and about those around them.

Gaming is for Everyone

We believe gaming is the next great frontier. According to PEW Research Center, 97% of teens already play video games. The diversity among the demographic has broken all previous stereotypes of age, gender, race and socioeconomic background. In short, gaming isn’t just for gamers anymore.

Adult-moderated
online environment

Hybrid is members-only, family-friendly, and monitored by background-checked adults who ensure a healthy community environment. Every student has gone through orientation and is aware of our rules and expectations, and our staff makes sure they're staying within these guidelines. If a member does require a note home, we can temporarily restrict access or even remove them entirely as a last resort. Peace of mind comes easier when you know your kids are playing with Hybrids.

Find your Tribe

The next generation is the first truly digital generation; they'll spend more time online than off. For most, their online journey begins with video games. That's where we come in: we provide early mentorship to produce better digital citizens for a better online world.

Gaming is as diverse as we are

We believe gaming, used appropriately, can be an incredible tool for learning, creative expression, social interaction, competition, entertainment and story-telling. And with so many games in so many genres, there’s a lot more to it than just the trendy game everyone is playing right now. So we built Hybrid to give kids a safe place to participate in their favorite pastime.
