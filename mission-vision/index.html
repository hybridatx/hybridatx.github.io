Mission and Vision

Almost all kids play video games. The truth is, we're all gamers. We want to show that being a gamer doesn't have to come with all the old, outdated stigma. 16 hours locked in a dark room, yelling at strangers online shouldn't be a normalized part of the experience. Candy, chips and energy drinks shouldn't be the prescribed diet given to our kids by advertisers the first time they pick up a controller.

It's time for change.

Hybrid's Mission:

Build a gaming brand that changes the paradigm of the industry and positively influences the lives of gamers.

Gaming culture, as it exists today, is in need of a complete overhaul. Hybrid does gaming better. We creates products, services and content that are designed to change the way we think about the world of gaming, and how that world influences its inhabitants. Whether you're a gamer for a year or a gamer for life, Hybrid wants to help you be your best self. Online and off.

Hybrid's Vision:

To improve society by inspiring our youth to live lives of complete wellness.

Hybrid is not a tech camp or a STEM camp. Hybrid is a youth development company that helps gamers become better people, for now and for the future. We believe the best way to have a positive impact is to meet them where they are. In 2021, that means digital spaces.
