A better community for gamers. For gamers who want a community built for them.

  • All online. Open for ages 13-17
  • Adult-monitored by background-checked staff

Hybrid Camps

Gaming and fitness, 5 days a week. For gamers who want something fun, social, and live!

  • Includes Hybrid Campus
  • All online. Open for ages 13-17
  • One week, Monday-Friday, 3 hours per day
  • Counselor-led gaming and fitness, live!
  • Add structure and exercise to your day
  • Meet new people to socialize and play with
  • Healthy, positive role models

Hybrid Envoys

"Like the Scouts for gamers". For in-game leaders who want to become their best selves; online and off.

  • Includes Hybrid Campus
  • Open for ages 13-17
  • Personal development, digital literacy and life skills
  • Earn badges in the 6 Hybrid-defining categories
  • Rank up to become an esteemed 'Platinum Hybrid'
  • Join a guild and create a culture all your own
  • Take on "Boss challenges" and win parts towards your own gaming PC
  • Looks great on applications to colleges, internships, jobs, and gaming orgs!

$300 per season
