Training the next
generation of gamers

The next generation is the first truly digital generation; they'll spend more time online than off. For most, their online journey begins with video games. That's where we come in: we provide early mentorship to produce better digital citizens for a better online world.

Joining is Easy

To become a Hybrid, just attend orientation! Here students learn the basics of what it means to be a Hybrid. They'll learn the rules and expectations of the community, meet other students, and play games alongside a Hybrid instructor.
